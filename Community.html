Slave Dancer Review by Kaitlyn: In the Slave Dancer, the main character,
Jesse lives in New Orlando, he knows how to play the fife, and use the fift to
catch attention of the sailors, and they could go into their town and buy things
from them, but he got kidnapped onto one of the ships. He had the rest of his
life in there until one day, one of the ship workers betrayed on captain, and
decided to help Jesse go home.
The 5th Wave Review by Ariel: There's no way around the clunky exposition
and rampant clichés that make up the majority of The 5th Wave. I really
enjoyed reading the book because it is exciting.
The First Phone Call from Heaven Review by Lauren: In a dark night, a boy
got a phone call from heaven he is afraid to pick up the phone. But he still pick
up and the phone call and it is from hell. I like this book because it has a lot of
twists and it’s interesting.
Harry Potter Series Review by Olivia: The Harry Potter series is written very
well and I enjoyed reading it. My favourite book out of the series is Harry
Potter and the Deathly Hallows because there are a lot of plot twists in the book.
Harry Potter is a wizard and he gets an acceptance letter to a magic school
called Hogwarts. The rest of the series continues about his life in Hogwarts.
